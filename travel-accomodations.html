Travel Accomodations

Whether you wish to bury your toes in the pearl-white sands of Roatan, or soar through the forests of La Tigra national park on a zipline, a comfy bed is must for your trip. In both largest cities, Tegucigalpa and San Pedro Sula, you can find centric hotels with complete attention to your needs from room service from their restaurants and airport pickup. If the purpose of your trip is to escape the buzz of the city, count on the following recommendations to find yourself in some of the best hospitality found in the country.

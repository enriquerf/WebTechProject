Important Info

It is extremely important for you to follow local guide recommendations. Honduras is a developing country where taking valuables around on the street might not be a good idea, especially for jewelry, watches, and phones! To ensure your safety, comfort, and peace of mind, I suggest you follow these tips:

  • Avoid showing valuables in public
  • Avoid traveling at nighttime
  • Do not carry your passport on you unless necessary
  • Use Uber! It is significantly safer than using public transport and traditional taxis
  • Take cash in Honduran Lempiras, not US$
  • Ask your local guide where to go, wandering off in the wrong part of town can quickly become unpleasant
  • Hire a driver for your stay. It is fairly cheap and allows for them to provide additional suggestions and safety tips

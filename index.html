After all, only a local can provide you with the latest and most relevant travel tips.

I wish to invite anyone with a curious mind to explore my country and its beauties. As in any country, a trip to Honduras might not be taken advantage of if you don't take in the full experience; like authentic food, activities, and simply being in the know of the culture and expectations.

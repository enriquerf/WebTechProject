Project Questions

  • What is your website about?
    • My website will focus on promoting travel to my home country, Honduras.
  • What is the objective of your website? (Also read as why would this site be important?)
    • I will aim to provide interested people with as much information as possible. Whether it is lodging/food recommendation, travel hotspots, or simply general facts about the country for possible visitors to review before traveling.
  • Who is your target audience?
    • My target audience will be 20-30 year olds with a sense of adventure and those who enjoy traveling to different destinations.
  • What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?
    • Popular Tourist Hotspots
    • Travel Accomodations
